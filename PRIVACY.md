# Privacy Policy — Mouse Tooltip Translator for Safari

**Last updated:** March 18, 2026

## What data is collected?

When you hover over or select text on a webpage, that text is sent to Google Translate (`translate.googleapis.com`) for translation.

## What data is stored?

- **Locally:** Translations are cached in memory during your browsing session for performance. The cache is cleared when you close the tab.
- **Remotely:** We do not store, log, or transmit any data to our own servers. We have no servers.

## What data is NOT collected?

- Passwords and form inputs (the extension ignores all input fields)
- Browsing history
- Personal information
- Analytics or usage data

## Third-party services

This extension uses Google Translate's public API. Google's privacy policy applies to the text sent for translation: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/achiya-automation/safari-tooltip-translator/issues).
